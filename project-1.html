CYBERNETIC VALKYRIE

CHARACTER DESIGN & SCULPTING

Project Overview

A high-fidelity 3D character sculpt exploring the fusion of organic anatomy and hard-surface cybernetics. The goal was to create a battle-ready Valkyrie for a sci-fi setting, focusing on functional armor mechanics and realistic skin rendering.

Tools Used: ZBrush, Maya, Substance Painter, Marmoset Toolbag
